The department runs 4-year full-time Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology (B.Tech. - IT) program with total intake of 180 students.

The department is working towards advanced technical enhancement and runs industry readiness programs through training, certification and development programs.
The department has signed MoUs with various industries and academic institutions such as ICT Academy, Palo Alto, Monteage IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd, Instrumentation Automation Surveillance & Communication (IASC) Sector Skill Council.
